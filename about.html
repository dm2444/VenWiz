About VenWiz

At VenWiz, we are building an online B2B platform for manufacturers and skilled service vendors to partner and prosper together. Despite the fact that B2B services are core to the manufacturing industry; the discovery, procurement and execution of these services in India has stayed underserved by digitization. Our idea is to onboard manufacturers and pre-screened vendors on a single platform across the lifecycle of service engagement. This includes an intuitive search platform to quicken and broaden the process of identifying the right vendors. An organized platform fosters transparency, accountability and collaboration, and we are excited to establish these traits in the Indian manufacturing ecosystem.
